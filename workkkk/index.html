Welcome to my page

Youth Alive is a club that was formed by our founder, a Catholic priest, Rev. Monsignor Alex Bobby Benson(Ph.D) who is currently attached to the Koforidua Diocese of the Catholic Church.

It was first formed in St Roses SHS since he was stationed in Akwatia at that time then to Pojoss and the other SHSs Presently, Youth Alive can be found in St Roses, Pojoss, Nifa, St Martins, Merries.

Fortunately old students of the club even upon reaching the university also join this workshop there by spreading the clubs reach to the universities and yet again efforts are in place to formally introduce university chapters of the club.The slogan, Youth ….. Be awake 2x Youth Alive….Active and Vibrant There is an anthem for the club as well.
